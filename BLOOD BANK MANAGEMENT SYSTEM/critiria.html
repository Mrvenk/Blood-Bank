Blood Donation Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for blood donation, you must meet the following criteria:

  • You must be at least 18 years old.
  • You must weigh at least 110 pounds (50 kg).
  • You should be in good general health.
  • You should not have donated blood in the last 8 weeks.
  • You should not have a cold, flu, or any other illness on the day of donation.
  • You should not have certain medical conditions or infections that can be transmitted through blood.

If you meet these criteria and are willing to donate blood, please visit your nearest blood donation center.
