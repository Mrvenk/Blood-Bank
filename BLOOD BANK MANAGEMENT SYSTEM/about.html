About Blood Bank Management System

The Blood Bank Management System is designed to efficiently manage blood donations, blood inventory, and donor information. This system helps blood banks streamline their operations and ensure a steady supply of blood to those in need.

Features of the Blood Bank Management System:

  • Donor registration and management
  • Blood inventory tracking and management
  • Request and distribution of blood units
  • Donor eligibility screening
  • Appointment scheduling for donors
  • Reporting and analytics
